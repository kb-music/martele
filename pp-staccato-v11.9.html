Practice Player — Help & Instructions

Practice Player is a focused music-study player built for efficient instrumental practice. It combines audio playback, score viewing, waveform navigation, loop points, speed control, and track-version switching in one clear workspace.

It is especially useful for repetition-based work: you can jump inside the waveform, repeat short passages, slow the music down, stop to the last playback start point, and switch between accompaniment or part versions without breaking concentration.

To explore other albums, open the main page. There you can learn about other albums.

TOOLTIPS:
Press T to cycle tooltip modes. SHOW ONES resets the remembered tips and shows them once again.
ALWAYS OFF hides all guided tips. SHOW ONES shows each tip once, then remembers it. ALWAYS ON shows tips every time you hover or focus a control.
